Features of Hybrid Flooring

Hybrid flooring is also resistant to extremes of heat and cold with minimal expansion or contraction. This means that even if your home has underfloor heating or constant temperature changes, your hybrid flooring will not buckle, lift or crack unlike other flooring products.

  • Limestone composite core — 100% waterproof and odour-proof
  • Noise-free, non-scratch, stain resistant — perfect for families
  • Resistant to temperature extremes — won't buckle, lift or crack
  • Compatible with underfloor heating systems
  • Click-lock floating installation — can go over existing floors
  • 4-Sided micro bevel for authentic plank appearance
  • UV coating for long-lasting surface protection
  • Acoustic Rating AAAC 6 Star
