Features of Classique Oak

Classique Oak is engineered for the Australian climate — the multi-layer plywood base resists seasonal movement while the real hardwood top layer delivers the authentic warmth and character of natural timber. Brushed and UV lacquered for lasting beauty.

  • Real 2mm hardwood top layer — genuine timber look and feel
  • Hardwood multi-layer plywood base — dimensionally stable
  • Brushed UV Lacquer surface — durable, natural-looking finish
  • 4-Sided Micro Bevel — authentic plank appearance
  • 2G Click System — floating or direct stick installation
  • FSC Certified · ATFA Member · ASI Accredited (ASI-ACC-026)
  • ≤25% random short boards — natural variation
